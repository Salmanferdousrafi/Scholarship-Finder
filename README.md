- Why AI Scholarship Finder?

Every year, millions of scholarships, internships, and research opportunities go undiscovered because students struggle to find reliable information.

AI Scholarship Finder solves this problem using Artificial Intelligence to match students with opportunities based on their academic background, interests, goals, country, and eligibility.

Instead of spending hours searching hundreds of websites, students receive personalized recommendations in seconds.
